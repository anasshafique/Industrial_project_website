OUR WORK

Project Overview

The Job Center currently receives approximately 5 million whitemail letters annually, over 25,000 documents daily. The manual process of reading and classifying these letters has led to significant operational challenges, including breached Service Level Agreements and delays in benefit processing, directly affecting vulnerable individuals relying on timely assistance.

THE SOLUTION

What We Are Building

Our proposed Whitemail Vulnerability Scanning Solution is a secure, serverless, AI-powered digital platform that ingests and digitizes whitemail through OCR technology, applies natural language processing and advanced AI classifiers for accurate classification, and detects and prioritizes vulnerable individuals to ensure they receive timely support.

WHO WE ARE

About Apex Secure Solutions

Apex Secure Solutions is a team of seven Computer Science students from the University of Sunderland, united by a shared passion for building technology that makes a real difference. Formed as part of the CET2004 Industrial Project module, we were brought together to tackle a genuine challenge faced by the Job Center, the overwhelming volume of whitemail correspondence that strains resources and delays support for vulnerable citizens.

We are committed to delivering a solution that is not only technically robust but also ethical, secure, and user-focused. The name Apex reflects our ambition, to reach the highest standard in everything we build.
